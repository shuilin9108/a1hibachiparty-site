If you are searching for outdoor hibachi catering, private hibachi chef near me, or backyard hibachi party FAQ, this page covers the most common questions about A1 Hibachi Party, including pricing, setup, protein options, travel fees, deposits, allergies, and booking details.

Pricing depends on guest count, package selected, premium upgrades, add-ons, travel distance, and applicable tax. A1 offers 2-protein, 3-protein, and large-party package options. Final pricing is confirmed after reviewing your event details.

A1 offers a 2-Protein Package, a 3-Protein Package, and a Large Party 2-Protein Package for events with 50+ guests. Package pricing and details are listed on the menu page.

A1 Hibachi Party is based in Brooklyn, NY and serves NYC, Upstate New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Pennsylvania. Service is generally available within about 3 hours of NYC depending on traffic, event size, and availability.

A1 Hibachi Party is focused on outdoor hibachi setups. Backyards, patios, driveways, terraces, and covered outdoor areas usually work best. Indoor cooking is generally not recommended unless the venue clearly allows it and safety conditions are confirmed.

Customers usually provide tables, chairs, plates, utensils, and a safe outdoor space for the chef to cook. The team will confirm setup details before the event.

The $50 deposit is optional. Paying the deposit can help secure your spot faster and give your booking priority scheduling. Final availability and pricing are still confirmed by the team.

The first 30 miles are included. After 30 miles, a $30 travel fee applies plus $1 per additional mile. Final travel cost may depend on event address, traffic, and scheduling.

Yes. Protein selection can be submitted during booking or deferred for later confirmation. Staff can update the order later if customers do not choose proteins immediately.

Regular proteins include Chicken, Shrimp, Steak, Salmon, Tofu, and Scallops. Premium upgrades include Filet Mignon and Lobster Tail. A1 Hibachi Party does not include squid.

Customers should list allergies and dietary requests during booking. Common allergy options include shellfish, peanuts, tree nuts, dairy, egg, soy, gluten, sesame, fish, and other. The team will review special needs before the event.

A birthday free meal promotion may apply when the order is over $800, the birthday is within 7 days of the event, and valid ID verification is provided. This promotion is not stackable with other promotions.

WELCOME50 may provide $50 off when active. Promotions generally cannot be stacked with other promotions, including the birthday free meal promotion.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, contact A1 Hibachi Party as early as possible. Policy details may depend on how close the request is to the event date, travel arrangements, and the size of the booking.
